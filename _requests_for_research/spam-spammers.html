--- title: 'Spam Spammers' summary: '' difficulty: 1.5 # out of 3 ---

Investigate use of language models to make spamming not-profitable

Spammers generate humongous amount of undesirable traffic and attention. Their email messages are annoying for most of people, but a small fraction of users follow their trap. Spammers receive responses from users extremely infrequently. Therefore, they manually reply to each of emails. The task is to build a bot that automatically replies to spam emails. Such bot shouldn't be easy to detect, and this could be achieved by use of a powerful language model. This system would be a great asset for humanity.
